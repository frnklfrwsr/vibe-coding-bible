# Code of Conduct

This repository expects evidence-based, constructive discussion.

Participants should:

- be respectful and direct
- separate facts, source-backed claims, field reports, and opinions
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, spam, and disruptive behavior
- avoid malicious or unsafe contribution attempts
- avoid presenting anecdotal field practice as official fact

Maintainers may close, edit, or remove contributions that are abusive, spammy, unsafe, misleading, or outside the repository's evidence and safety standards.
